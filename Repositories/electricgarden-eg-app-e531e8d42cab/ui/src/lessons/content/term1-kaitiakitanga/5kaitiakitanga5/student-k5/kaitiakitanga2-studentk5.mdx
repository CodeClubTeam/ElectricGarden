### **Data for Mauri**

<Captioned>
    <b>Ngā raraunga mō te mauri</b>
</Captioned>

##### Data can link with the real world to help us care for the **mauri** of our taonga.

##### Mauri is **life essence** that we help to care for with our actions as tuākana, tēina, and kaitiaki.

##### To help us think abour mauri, we can think about it on the specturm of this **mauri-o-meter**:

<br />

<Figure>
    <ImageWithBorder src={mauriometer} border="true" />
</Figure>

###### _**Note:** Based on the Mauri Model Decision Making Framework (MMDMF)_

<br />

###### This scale is a tool for measuring how our decisions and actions **impact the life essence** of the world around us.

###### For example, a reading of **0** on the mauri-o-meter means that the life force is sustained.

###### Then, depending on what we do, we can **support** (+1, +2) or **impair** (-1, -2) the strength of that force.

<br />
